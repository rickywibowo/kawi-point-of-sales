# Stage 80 - Modern Accounting Foundation

Status: complete.

## Direction

KAWI POS accounting is now Laravel + Filament first. Vue/Nuxt remains paused.

This stage establishes the accounting foundation before continuing product/category work.

## Accounting Model

- Chart of Accounts is scoped per business.
- Outlet/branch is an accounting dimension, not a duplicated chart of accounts.
- Journal entries carry `business_id` and `outlet_id`.
- Journal entry lines carry `account_id`, `debit`, and `credit`.
- Profit and loss per outlet can be generated by filtering `journal_entries.outlet_id`.
- Opening balance is represented as a posted journal entry with `source_type = opening_balance`.
- Cash/bank behavior per outlet is mapped through `outlet_account_mappings`.

## Seeded COA

Basic COA is created for every existing business with `updateOrCreate`.

Core accounts include:

- Cash, Bank, QRIS Receivable, Inventory
- Accounts Payable
- Owner Capital, Opening Balance Equity
- Sales Food, Sales Beverage, Sales Other
- COGS Food, COGS Beverage
- Rent, Salary, Utilities, Payment Fee, Cash Shortage
- Other Income, Cash Overage

Default outlet mappings are created for each outlet:

- `cash` -> Cash
- `bank` -> Bank
- `qris_receivable` -> QRIS Receivable
- `cash_shortage` -> Cash Shortage Expense
- `cash_overage` -> Cash Overage Income
